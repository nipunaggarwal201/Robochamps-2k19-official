Amphibot

Problem Statement

The Indian Armed Forces is involved in a mission on the border of India and Bangladesh to kill terrorists as they have received an intel, but they have problem in crossing the water areas and uneven land.

"Design a prototype vehicle that can move in water, and on land to help them complete their mission and return safely without any difficulty."

Description

Design and make a wired or wirelessly controlled bot, which has to navigate through land and water obstacles, in the shortest time possible. The bot can be powered either by batteries or power supply.

Advantages

  • Low maintenance.
  • Easy to use.
  • No marina or storage costs.
  • It is used by military forces to secure the sea borders from increasing conflicts via sea.
  • It can help in transportation over muddy/ marshy area.
  • It is much helpful in surveillance and rescue operations in disasters like floods.

Bot Specifications

  • Robot can be wired or wireless.
  • maximum dimensions of bot should not be more than 30cm X 30cm (L X B) . Height can be adjusted as per convenience
  • The weight of the robot including all the accessories and equipment excluding the power source should not be more than 3.5 kgs
  • weight should be balanced such that the bot can float on water
  • A power supply should be maximum 9 to 12 volts.
  • If bot is wired than length of wire should be long enough to cover whole track
  • Bot motors and connections should be covered properly such that there shouldn't be any mishap when bot would be in water
  • Empahasise more on light weight materials
  • Maximum DC source voltage allowed is 12V.
