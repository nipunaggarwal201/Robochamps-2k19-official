Hackatronics

Problem Statement

In industry and army, circuit debugging plays an important role, having partial knowledge of which may lead to lose of life.

It is of great importance in army as well as in industries for fault finding and rectifying circuits as per requirements.

"The event testing the basic electronic knowledge and circuit debugging of the candidate may help him/her to be industrial ready."

Description

The event is based on examining the theoretical knowledge of the candidates and applying it to debug a circuit. The candidate has to qualify various rounds in order to prove his/her theoretical knowledge and apply it in order to rectify a circuit. The event is concerned with the basic electronic knowledge quiz and circuit debugging which test the candidate understanding of theorical knowledge and its applications in real life.

Its industrial application is that it can help in defusing bombs etc at a dangerous area in military applications. Another application is that it may help in finding and rectifying circuit in industry and military application.

Advantages

  • The event helps in testing the theoretical knowledge of the candidate.
  • The event helps in bridging up the gap between candidates theoretical knowledge and practical applications.
