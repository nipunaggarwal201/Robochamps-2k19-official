Robo Transpo

Problem Statement

" Arena is designed as per the circumstances in a war zone consisting of fire, rocks, sand, water, enemies, etc. Enviornment conditions would be tough. The bot should be designed in a way that it should take minimum time, escaping the intrusion of enemies and other physical factors. Bot should transport the load to desired destination."

Description

A bot will be graded according to the way of transportating the food, weapons etc to the soilders in the war conditioned arena in minimum time. The bot should be able to tackle all the possible huddles in a war prone area.

Its industrial application is wide It can be used as a robo to replace humans, being accomplishing the tasks which are beyond human capabilities such as bomb planting , bomb defusing, transporting essentials through a war prone area.

Advantages

  • Basic aim behind it is to make army more equipped in terms of techonolgy and safety.
  • By this transportation done fast in tough conditions.

Bot Specifications

  • Robot Can Be Wired or Wireless.
  • Machine Should Fit In The Box Of 300mm X 300mm X 300mm (L X B X H).
  • The weight of the machine including all the accessories and equipment excluding the power source should be no more than 2.5 kg.
  • In case of wireless robot weight of power source must be included.
  • Any types of pneumatics or hydraulics or fire weapons are NOT allowed.
  • Only DC power source is allowed and the voltage must not exceed 12V.
  • Robot should be capable of picking and placing the cube of max 6cm x 6cm x 6cm.
  • Machine should not be made from any ready-made assembly kits, Lego parts.
