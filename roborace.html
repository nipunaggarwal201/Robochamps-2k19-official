Roborace

Problem Statement

It's a well acknowledged fact that for an army mobility and reach within a challenging terrain like the Himalayas is super essential to gain an upper hand over their enemies.

"In order to accomplish that goal a preliminary requirement is a fast moving vehicle which can transport troops and goods over challenging terrains within a flash."

Description

You have to construct a bot (wired/wireless) whose task is to complete a race course with multiple obstacles within the least time possible without using any sort of mechanical engine or preinstalled market kits / lego parts.

The event will be executed in three rounds with increased difficulty level with the commencement of each round.

The bot has multiple industrial applications and can be used in most of the industries after implementing minor modifications. The bot can be utilised to transport general goods in geographically isolated areas Bot can also work to shift heavy goods within an industry. With minor modifications bot can be also be used for surveillance and law enforcement by the army and police.

Advantages

  • Solve Transportation Problem:

    • The event would help budding engineers understand the future needs of transportation which would encourage them to find new ideas and solutions towards the problem. The bot making will also promote interest in robotics and electronics among students.

Bot Specifications

  • Robot Can Be Wired or Wireless.
  • Machine Should Fit In The Box Of 250mm X 250mm X 250mm (L X B X H).
  • The weight of the machine including all the accessories and equipment excluding the power source should be no more than 3 kg.
  • In case of wireless robot weight of power source must be included.
  • Any types of pneumatics or hydraulics or fire weapons are NOT allowed.
  • Only DC power source is allowed and the voltage must not exceed 12V.
  • Lego kits are not allowed in any form.
