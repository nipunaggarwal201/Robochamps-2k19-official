Robowar / Minirobowar

Problem Statement

In light of the recent events that took place in Pulwama and our government’s action to those events, we as a country witnessed yet another unmatched performance by our defence system. The defence force has lived upto its expectations and it is high time we do too.

Its time to take the fight to a new level of technology. As the technological brains of our Nation it is on our shoulders to build technologies specifically WarBots that in the near future can replace the loss of life. Lets take a pledge to not let any other Case like Abhinandan Thakur happen to our Nation.

"So your task, should you choose to accept it, is to Design and Manufacture a Manually Controlled Robot(WarBot) Wired or Wireless within the specified Dimensions stated below. The WarBot should be capable of fighting, damaging, demolishing and completely immobilising or if instructed to then completely destroying the opponent without failure. The WarBot should be able to withstand any brutal force or obstacle that may come in between its target. The Technology implemented must be so Advanced and Effective that the WarBot should be in and out of the mission before the opponent can even gather intell of the task our defence has assigned to the WarBot."

Description

Long gone are the days of War when two nations would Fight for Glory. This is the new era, the era of automation, the era of Science. The ERA of Robots. It is a very proud moment for this nation when the gennext of technology starts to take responsibility to build WarBots that will make a new benchmark on how Wars will be fought in the future. Have you ever wondered what would it be like to put a WarBot to test to face another WarBot head on. Who would win? Who will Survive the Battle? Are you the one who will Go Home with the Glory?

So grab your tools and let your horses run Wild. For We, at the ROBOWARS 2k19, are ready to make these Imaginations turn into Reality, with the Best in Class Arena and Top-Class Robots from all over India Combating against each other. Are You ready to feel the Rush? Do you have what it takes to beat the Best of the Best? Or will Your Bot Bow down Crumpled to Pieces. Well there is only one way to find out…

The WarBot is versatile in nature and can be used for surveillance and gathering of intel or can be loaded with weapons to fight actual wars. The technology of WarBot is not only limited to its use in Defence. The technology has uses in mining, sea exploration and construction too

Advantages

  • The WarBot will in the near future replace the need of a soldier to put himself at risk.
  • The WarBot can be easily mass produced.
  • There is no training required for multiple bots to work in synchronisation only coding is required for that purpose.

Bot Specifications Robowar

  • Robot can be wired or wireless.
  • Robot should fit in a box of 750mm X 750mm X 750mm (L X B X H).
  • The weight of the robot including all the accessories and equipment excluding the power source should be no more than 60 lbs.
  • In case of wireless robot weight of power source must be included.
  • Any types of pneumatics or hydraulics or fire weapons are NOT allowed.
  • Thickness of the material which is used as an armor to protect parts of bot should not exceed 5mm.
  • Only DC power source is allowed and the voltage must not exceed 25V.

Bot Specifications Minirobowar

  • Robot can be wired or wireless.
  • Robot should fit in a box of 500mm X 500mm X 500mm (L X B X H).
  • The weight of the robot including all the accessories and equipment excluding the power source should be no more than 30 lbs.
  • In case of wireless robot weight of power source must be included.
  • Any types of pneumatics or hydraulics or fire weapons are NOT allowed.
  • Only DC power source is allowed and the voltage must not exceed 25V.
